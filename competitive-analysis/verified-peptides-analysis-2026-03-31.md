## STRATEGIC OPTIONS ANALYSIS

### Option 1: Premium Positioning (HOLD)
**Maintain current $105–$190 pricing**

**Pros:**
- 86–95% unit margins = $60–$180 gross profit per unit
- Signals quality, trustworthiness, expertise
- Attracts high-LTV customers (willing to pay for premium)
- Funding fuel for aggressive marketing spend (10–20% ROAS achievable)

**Cons:**
- Smaller addressable market (assumes 20–30% of buyers)
- Requires consistent brand reinforcement
- Verified Peptides can undercut aggressively

**Breakeven:** 1 premium sale = 2–3 budget sales in profit dollars
**Recommendation:** STRONG FIT if brand positioning is locked in

---

### Option 2: Volume Play (AGGRESSIVE)
**Cut prices 15–25% to compete on price**

**Price Example:**
- BPC-157: $105 → $80 (-24%) | Still maintain ~76% margin
- NAD+: $190 → $160 (-16%) | Still maintain ~94% margin
- MOTS-C: $110 → $90 (-18%) | Still maintain ~84% margin

**Pros:**
- Instantly captures price-sensitive buyers (50–60% of market)
- Higher transaction volume = better fulfillment efficiency
- Crushes Verified Peptides on unit price (still 30–50% premium)
- Still maintain 75–90%+ margins

**Cons:**
- Dilutes premium brand positioning
- Increases fulfillment volume → operational strain
- Margin compression per unit = need 2x volume to match current revenue
- Market saturation risk if multiple competitors adopt same strategy

**Breakeven:** Need 2–2.5x transaction volume to match current gross profit $
**Recommendation:** RISKY without operational readiness for 2–3x fulfillment volume

---

### Option 3: Hybrid Scaling (RECOMMENDED)
**Maintain premium on hero products, introduce budget tier**

**Structure:**
```
PREMIUM TIER (Current):
  • BPC-157, NAD+, MOTS-C at $105–$190
  • Margins: 89–95%
  • Positioning: Premium peptides, research-grade quality

BUDGET TIER (New):
  • Same peptides, lower positioning
  • Prices: $65–$95 (20–25% discount vs premium)
  • Margins: Still 70–85% (exceptional)
  • Target: Price-conscious repeat buyers

EXAMPLE:
  BPC-157 Premium: $105 | 89% margin
  BPC-157 Budget:  $79  | 75% margin (still $67.80 gross per unit)
```

**Pros:**
- Captures BOTH premium and price-conscious segments
- Maintains brand equity on hero products
- Budget tier still delivers 70–85% margins (not a race to zero)
- Higher overall transaction volume without cannibalizing premium
- Buildable operational model (existing fulfillment infrastructure)

**Cons:**
- Requires separate SKU management, marketing messaging
- Risk of premium customers trading down to budget tier
- Messaging needs to be precise (avoid brand confusion)

**Breakeven:** 40% premium mix + 60% budget mix = 82% blended margin  
**Recommendation:** BEST STRATEGIC FIT

## REVENUE OPTIMIZATION STRATEGIES

### Strategy 1: Margin Stacking
**Layer operational expenses strategically across high-margin products**

```
Revenue per order: $150 (2–3 units)
Wholesale COGS: ~$20–30 (blended)
Fulfillment + Labor: $12
Packaging + Branding: $3
Merchant Fees (3.5%): $5.25
Platform/Email/Analytics: $2
Total OpEx: ~$22.25

Gross Profit: $150 - $50.25 = $99.75 (66% margin)
```

**Action:** Use high-margin products (NAD+, MOTS-C) to subsidize fulfillment on lower-margin items (Tesamorelin). Still maintain 81%+ unit margins.

---

### Strategy 2: AOV Stacking
**Increase average order value to spread fulfillment cost**

Current: 1 unit orders @ $100–150 avg = $12 fulfillment burn = 8–12% of revenue  
Optimized: 2–3 unit orders @ $200–400 avg = $12 fulfillment burn = 3–6% of revenue  

**Tactics:**
- Bundle products (BPC-157 + TB-500 = $175, save 8%)
- Tiered discounts (Buy 2 get 10% off)
- Subscription (Monthly auto-replenish = 15% discount)
- Loyalty multipliers (5% off when reach $500 LTV)

**Impact:** 2x AOV = halve fulfillment as % of revenue = higher net margins

---

### Strategy 3: LTV Maximization
**Focus on repeat customers (90% of revenue)**

Verified Peptides' pricing attracts one-time bargain hunters.  
Elixser's margin structure supports loyalty programs that Verified can't match.

```
First order discount: -15% (acquire at $89.25, absorb margin hit)
Repeat order margin: Standard 89–95%
3+ orders: +5% loyalty bonus on margin
```

**Example:**
- Order 1: $105 → $89.25 | 73% margin (takes a hit)
- Order 2: $105 | 89% margin (recovered)
- Order 3+: $105 + 5% loyalty = premium service

**Impact:** Repeat customer LTV = 3–4x first-time buyer, still 85%+ blended margins
