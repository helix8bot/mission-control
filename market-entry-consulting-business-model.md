## INTERVIEW FRAMEWORK FOR WARM LEADS

### Qualifying Conversation (30 min)

**Questions to Ask:**

1. **Commitment Level**
   - What's your timeline for launch? (Answer: If not within 6 months, too early)
   - Do you have capital ready? (Answer: Minimum $50K liquid, committed)
   - Are you doing this full-time or side project? (Answer: Full-time preferred)

2. **Market Understanding**
   - What peptides are you planning to sell? (Answer: Should have specific ideas, not vague)
   - Who's your target customer? (Answer: Should be specific — researchers, athletes, longevity community, etc.)
   - Why do you think you can win vs competitors? (Answer: Looks for differentiation thinking)

3. **Business Experience**
   - Have you run a business before? (Background context, not filter)
   - What's your marketing background? (Helps assess advisory needs)
   - What's your technical ability? (WooCommerce setup, GHL understanding)

4. **Blockers**
   - What's your biggest concern right now? (Filters to right package)
   - What would make this easier? (Identifies specific pain)
   - Are you more worried about suppliers, compliance, or marketing? (Tier recommendation)

5. **Fit Assessment**
   - Would you be comfortable with "research use only" positioning? (Non-negotiable for compliance)
   - Are you willing to follow the playbook vs. improvise? (Executes faster)
   - Can you move fast when needed? (Timeliness matters for market window)

**Red Flags:**
- ❌ Wants to position as "supplement for humans" (compliance risk)
- ❌ No capital ready, wants to bootstrap with business loan (funding risk)
- ❌ Already talked to "other consultants" (shopping price, not serious)
- ❌ Vague on target market or differentiation (unlikely to succeed)
- ❌ "Just want to resell existing products" (race to bottom, low margins)

**Green Lights:**
- ✅ Clear product idea + target market
- ✅ Capital ready to invest ($50K+)
- ✅ Timeline: 3-6 months to launch
- ✅ Willing to follow proven playbook
- ✅ Serious about compliance/legal positioning
