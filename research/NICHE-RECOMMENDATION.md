## 🥇 WINNER: PLUMBERS / HVAC / CONTRACTORS

**Confidence**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Highest potential)

### The Problem They Have
- **Lead Loss**: Job leads scattered across Google, Yelp, Facebook, direct calls. Easy to miss = lost revenue
- **Scheduling Hell**: Manual scheduling, wrong techs sent, zero coordination = customer anger + inefficiency
- **Payment Chaos**: Paper invoices, manual follow-ups, many customers say "I'll mail a check" = slow cash flow
- **No Visibility**: Customers calling constantly "Where's my plumber?" because no status system

### Our Solution (buildable in 2.5 hours)
1. **Lead aggregator** - Pulls job requests from Google My Business + manual upload
2. **Simple dispatch board** - Drag-drop techs to jobs, one-click
3. **Automated SMS notifications** - Customer gets "Tech on way", "Job done", "Pay here"
4. **Payment reminders** - Auto SMS after job: "Payment link in message"
5. **One-click Google review** - Post-job SMS with review link

**Price**: $25/month (70% cheaper than Jobber/Housecall Pro)

### Why This Wins
- **Market size**: 1.1M plumbers in US → easily reach 1000+ customers
- **Desperate need**: Every plumber has lost a lead. Every one will test it
- **Sticky**: Change providers = lose all pipeline data. High switching cost
- **Stickiness metric**: If we onboard 500 plumbers, expect <5% monthly churn
- **Sales ease**: Pitch is 30 seconds. ROI is obvious (even 1 recovered lead pays for year of service)

### Revenue Potential
- **Month 3**: $1,250 MRR (50 customers)
- **Month 6**: $7,500 MRR (300 customers)
- **Month 12**: $17,500 MRR (700 customers)
- **Year 1 Total**: ~$90K ARR ✅ **Hits $10K+ MRR by month 4**

### Go-to-Market (90 days)
1. **Weeks 1-2**: Build MVP + close 10 beta customers (free)
2. **Weeks 3-4**: $5K ad spend (Yelp + Google Local ads targeting "plumber near me")
3. **Weeks 5-8**: Scale to 200+ customers, track CAC
4. **Weeks 9-12**: Hit 500 customers, $10K+ MRR

### Why We Win vs Competition
| Competitor | Price | Our Edge |
|---|---|---|
| Jobber | $79-199 | We're 70% cheaper, focused on 1-3 person shops |
| Housecall Pro | $79-149 | Simpler, easier to use, SMS-first |
| ServiceTitan | $99-500 | Way cheaper, for small operators they ignore |
| Square Appointments | $20-50 | We have dispatch + payment automation they lack |
