## KEY INSIGHTS FROM RESEARCH

### 1. The Gap in the Market
- ServiceTitan ($99-500/mo), Housecall Pro ($79-149/mo), Jobber ($79-199/mo) — all too expensive for solo/2-person shops
- **The gap**: Solo plumbers ($150K-400K revenue) can't justify $100/mo but desperately need automation
- **Our edge**: $25/month, focused on top 3 problems, SMS-first, simple UX

### 2. The Revenue Model Works
- **CAC**: $50-100 per customer (via ads + word-of-mouth)
- **LTV**: $25/mo × 97% retention × 36 months = $720 per customer
- **LTV:CAC**: 7:1 (excellent for SaaS)
- **Payback period**: 2 months

### 3. The Switching Costs Are High
- Once a plumber imports their pipeline into our system, they can't leave without losing data
- Competitors know this — they don't bother competing on price (they target scale)
- We can own the small operator segment without competition

### 4. The Sales Cycle is Fast
- Plumber sees demo → realizes immediate ROI (even recovering 1 lead pays for a year)
- No procurement, no committee → decision maker is owner
- Can close via demo call or free trial
- Expected sales cycle: 3-5 days
