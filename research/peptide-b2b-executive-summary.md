## COMPARISON: B2B Services vs. Product Launch (PeptideLaunch)

**PeptideLaunch (Product Business):**
- Requires content creation (guides, email sequences, VSLs)
- Requires funnel building (landing page, copywriting, ad testing)
- Margin: 50-80% (high)
- Scaling: Limited by content production speed
- Timeline: 2-3 months to revenue

**Peptide B2B Services (Service Business):**
- Requires documented playbooks (not content creation)
- Requires client acquisition (warm outreach, not ads)
- Margin: 60-80% (comparable to PeptideLaunch)
- Scaling: Limited by time (max 5-10 clients)
- Timeline: 4-6 weeks to first revenue
- **Advantage:** Can run parallel to PeptideLaunch without conflict

**Recommendation:** These are complementary, not competing. B2B services can fund PeptideLaunch launch.
