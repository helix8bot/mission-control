## SECTION 1: Current Peptide B2B Landscape

### Market Context
- **Industry stage:** Peptide DTC market is early but consolidating (FDA compliance increasing)
- **Supplier concentration:** Fewer than 10 major suppliers in the US (p4R, Peptide Sciences, Canadian Peptides, etc.)
- **Demand signal:** Hundreds of new entrants annually trying to start peptide companies
- **Barrier to entry:** Supplier access + compliance knowledge + customer acquisition
- **Market pressure:** FDA regulatory uncertainty forcing better compliance practices

### Competitive Services Already Existing
- Peptide broker services (markup on raw materials)
- Compliance consulting (typically $5K-$50K project fees)
- Full funnel/marketing agencies (generic, not peptide-specific)
- Dropshipping/white-label peptide suppliers
- Ad agencies (no peptide expertise)

**Gap:** No one is offering peptide-specific marketing + supplier network access + market entry guidance as a unified service.
