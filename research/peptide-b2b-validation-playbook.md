## PHASE 1: DEMAND VALIDATION (Week 1-2)

**Goal:** Confirm peptide companies struggle with Meta ad account bans and would pay for expertise

#### Where to Find Prospects
- **Reddit:** r/peptides, r/Peptidebros, r/sourcing
- **Discord:** Peptide research communities (invite-only but accessible)
- **Twitter:** #peptide, #research chemicals, #SARMs (overlap audience)
- **LinkedIn:** Search "founder" + "peptide" or "research peptide" in title

```
Title: "Meta Account Suspension + Peptide Ads? I've figured out how to keep them compliant"

Post:

the account compliant.

- What language triggers Meta's peptide filters

- Account setup that reduces ban risk by 80%+

Interested? Drop a comment below. No sales pitch, just knowledge share.
```

#### Outreach Message (LinkedIn/Twitter - Direct)
```
Subject: "Peptide ads that don't get banned"

Body:
```

---

## REDFLAGS TO STOP & PIVOT

| RedFlag | Action |
|---|---|
| <5 people interested in Meta ads service | Adjust messaging; use stronger case study data; or deprioritize |
| No one will pay $3K+/month for ads service | Service model doesn't work; position as lower-priced % of spend instead |
| Entrepreneurs don't want $7.5K consulting | Offer smaller $2-3K "Market Entry Blueprint" instead; save full consulting for later |
| Suppliers won't confirm referral fees | Referral model may not be viable; skip for now |
| 0 commitments by Week 4 | Messaging/positioning issue; take 1-week pause to refine offer |
